About Kamadenu Goushala

Our story, mission, and the people behind this seva.

Our Story

Kamadenu Goushala began as a small shelter for just a few abandoned cows. Over the years, with the blessings of the divine and the support of kind-hearted devotees, it has grown into a loving home for dozens of desi cows, bulls, and calves.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide lifelong protection, food, medical care, and respect to every cow that enters our shelter.

Vision: A society where every cow is treated as family, not as a product; where gau-seva becomes a natural part of daily life.

Founder

Sri Ramesh Sharma, a devoted gau-bhakta, started Kamadenu Goushala after witnessing the suffering of abandoned cows on city streets. Leaving his corporate job, he dedicated his life completely to gau-seva and rural development.
